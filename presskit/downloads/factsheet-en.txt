Hero Wing Official Fact Sheet

Title: Hero Wing
Genre: Side-scrolling flying action game
Platform: Android (Google Play)
Release: July 2026
Developer / Publisher: Paper Boat Games
Price: Free to play (optional IAP and opt-in rewarded ads; no forced ads)
Languages: Korean, English, Japanese, Traditional Chinese, German, French and Brazilian Portuguese

One-line description
A side-scrolling flying action game built around Near Miss Combos, full-gauge Invincible Dash and stage boss battles.

About the game
Hero Wing is a mobile action game about managing speed, avoiding obstacles and flying as far as possible while health constantly drains. Passing close without contact triggers a Near Miss and builds combo and score bonuses. Your Dashes and Near Misses fill the gauge, unleashing Invincible Dash for a few seconds at full charge, while stage bosses add a new rhythm to each flight.

Key features
- 12 playable characters and pet progression
- Near Miss Combos, Dash and Invincible Dash
- Stage boss battles
- Google Play achievements and cloud save
- Core stages support offline play
- Seven supported languages
